the department of quality control

meet moose.

Every scruffyboy product is developed on and around Moose — a real dog with a rare talent for finding mud in dry weather. He is not a mascot. He will not be turned into a cartoon.

The standard is simple: if a product doesn't hold up after his worst afternoon, it doesn't ship.

“i built this for the dirty dogs who love to play but also love the couch.”— tom, founder

why we're making this

the pet aisle wasn't listening.

It's wall-to-wall paw prints and puns, while your dog sits in the boot, wet, redecorating. We make serious cleanup gear instead — premium formulas, quick formats, built for the time between grooms. No baby talk. It works.
