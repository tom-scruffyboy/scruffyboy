meet moose.

Every scruffyboy product is developed on and around Moose — a real dog with a rare talent for finding mud in dry weather. He is not a mascot. He will not be turned into a cartoon.

The standard is simple: if a product doesn't hold up after his worst afternoon, it doesn't ship.
